Students lifehacks

What Aber students should better know to save up some cash
  1. Travel free throughout Wales with "Traws Cymru" bus on weekends
  2. Benefit from your being a student and use your student ID anywhere you can. It is accepted in many restaurants and shops
  3. Save up on food and drinks by going to uni events
  4. Persuade your department to go to a trip, they might offer you lots of discounts and consession prices
  5. Use loyalty cards-they always provide vouchers and extra discounts
  6. Don't rush to purchase books, you can always borrow it in the library
  7. Don't rush to buy things, Black Friday, Christmas and End of Season sales are always there
  8. "Turn your trash into cash"-Sell unnecessary stuff online
  9. Save money by investing in waterbottle
  10. Always have some spare cash for emergency situations or for exciting opportunities
